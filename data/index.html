Literature

Most of the cited literature except some books and monographs can be found in the literature folder of the repository of interest. The publications are titled with format: first four letters of author last name last two digits of publication year and part of the title (eg. MacIver, Malcolm A., Noura M. Sharabash, and Mark E. Nelson. "Prey-capture behavior in gymnotid electric fish: motion analysis and effects of water conductivity." *Journal of experimental biology* 204.3 (2001): 543-557. in pdf form is: MacI01a_prey_capture_behavior_gymanotid_el_4500.pdf)
