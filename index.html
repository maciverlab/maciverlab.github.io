This is the website for MacIver Lab led by Malcom MacIver.
We are part of the Neuroscience and Robotics (NxR) Lab based in Northwestern University, Evanston.
This site has links to on-going research , computional projects , and available data .



We study animal behavior from ethological, evolutionary, neuronal, robotic and computational angles
to understand how the brain processes complex stimuli in the generation of motor programs .

A list of publications from this lab can be found at the NxR website.
